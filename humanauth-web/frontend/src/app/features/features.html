HumanAuth Features

Advanced Face Detection

Our system uses MediaPipe's face landmark detection to identify and track 468 distinct points on the human face in real-time, ensuring the presence of a genuine human rather than a photo or mask.

👋

Interactive Hand Challenges

Random hand gesture challenges require users to perform specific movements, making it virtually impossible for bots to simulate human behavior or for attackers to use pre-recorded videos.

Simple API Integration

Integrate HumanAuth into your applications with our straightforward REST API. Secure your forms, login processes, and sensitive actions with human verification that's more reliable than traditional CAPTCHA.
